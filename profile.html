"Wonder of Srilankan Wildlife"

Sri Lanka is a wildlife enthusiast's paradise and has plenty to offer visitors interested in animal and birdlife. This tropical island harbours an incredible selection of flora and fauna and is home to some of the finest national parks of the subcontinent. Home to a variety of species including the elusive leopard, regal elephant, blue whale, and a whole host of exotic birds, below is a list of the best places to see diverse and exciting wildlife on a tailor-made holiday to Sri Lanka.

  • Leopard (Panthera pardus kotiya)
  • Asian Elephant (Elephas maximus maximus)
  • Sloth Bear (Melursus ursinus)
  • Spotted Deer (Axis axis)
